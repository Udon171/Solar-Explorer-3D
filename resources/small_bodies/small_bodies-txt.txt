Small bodies in the solar system include asteroids, comets, and meteoroids. They are remnants from the early solar system and can be found mainly in the asteroid belt and Kuiper belt.

Key Facts:
- Asteroids: Rocky objects, mostly in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter
- Comets: Icy bodies that develop tails when near the Sun
- Meteoroids: Small rocky or metallic bodies traveling through space
- Some small bodies have moons or are binary systems
